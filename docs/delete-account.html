Delete Your Account

Last Updated: January 2025

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the LogMyLog app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (usually accessible from the main menu or profile section)
  3. Find the "Account" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account" or "Request Account Deletion"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your deletion request
  6. Confirm your identity if prompted (for security purposes)

Alternative Method: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, please send an email to:

Email: privacy@logmylog.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your username or account identifier
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  • Any additional information that may help us locate your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your user account, username, and authentication credentials
  • Health Tracking Data: All your poop logs, including timestamps, types, feelings, locations, duration, wipe counts, and flush information
  • Photos: All photos you've uploaded, including photos attached to logs and CrapChat posts
  • Social Data: Your CrapChat groups, posts, messages, and friend connections
  • Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and theme preferences
  • Statistics: All calculated statistics, streaks, and achievements
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens used for push notifications

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and operational purposes, some data may be retained for a limited period:

  • Transaction Records: Purchase history and payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes (as required by law)
  • Moderation Records: Reports or moderation actions may be retained for up to 90 days for safety and security purposes
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics purposes

Data Deletion Timeline

Once you submit a deletion request:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated and you will no longer be able to access the app
  • Within 30 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our active systems
  • Within 90 days: Data will be removed from backup systems (if applicable)

Note: Some data may take longer to delete from third-party services (Supabase, Adapty) depending on their data retention policies.

Third-Party Services

Your data is stored with the following third-party services. When you delete your account, we will request deletion from:

  • Supabase: Your database records and stored files
  • Adapty: Your subscription and purchase history

Please note that third-party services may have their own data retention policies and deletion timelines.

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to log in to your account
  • All your logs, photos, and social content will be permanently removed
  • You will be removed from all CrapChat groups
  • Your friend connections will be removed
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again in the future
